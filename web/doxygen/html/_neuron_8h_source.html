13 @details Synaptic plasticity modifies the strength of a synapse temporarily
14  depending on the activity of the neuron.
15 
16 @par Types of synaptic plasticity @parblock
17  There are two types of synaptic plasticity depending on their mechanics and the time required to return the Synapse to it´s baseline strength:\n
18  -Short-term plasticity (STP): acts as a band-pass filter of the pre-synaptic activity, is added to the base line strength of the synapse.\n
19  -Long-term plasticity (LTP): uses hebbian learning and it´s intensity depends on the phase shift of the post-synaptic activity respect to the pre-synaptic activity, is added to the baselinestrengh of the synapse.
20 
21 @endparblock
22 
23 @par Epigenetics @parblock
24  Epigenetics are modelled as inheritable changes in the strength of a connection
25  related to external factors (fitness of the Neural Network).
26 @endparblock
27 
28 @par Relationships with other classes @parblock
29  The Synapse class is the base class of PlasticSynapse used in the vectors of
30  synapses by type for each Neuron, all the types of synapses are implemented
31  in the SpikingEvaluator class wich evauates all Neurons in a NeuralNetwork.
32 @endparblock
